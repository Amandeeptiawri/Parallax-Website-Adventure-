PARA GLIDING

Set yourself free for Paragliding in Nandi Hills to enjoy amidst the rocky valleys, blue sky, and lush greenery. Enjoy the breathtaking 360-degree panorama from a height of 4000 feet that reduces everything to tiny spots.

Soar high in the sky for 10-15 minutes and enjoy the empty vastness of the skies with the winds rushing around you. Feel safe as the activity is undertaken under the supervision of Skilled Professionals and with the best protective gear.
